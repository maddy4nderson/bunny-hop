How do we work?

BunnyHop uses a few different ways of getting started. You can easily scan the QR codes found on the bike to set up your account via the app. We work as a loyality program in which you can use your account online to top up your spending. We also offer a loyality card so that you cna top up a physical card. This means that our bikes aren't limited to card only users. We understand that sometimes the use of card only options can be retricting for some peoples use, and here at BunnyHop we are actively trying get as many minds involved.
