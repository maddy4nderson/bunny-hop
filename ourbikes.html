The Good Old-Fashioned Manual Bike

We offer the standard bike as well as electric to allow for as many people to get involved as we can. Riding a bike is an easy way to maintain a healthy lifestyle and keep fit. Our manual bikes are fitted with gears to ensure the route you decide to follow is best equipt for. These bikes are also set at a cheaper price than the electric so that people of all incomes have a chance to contribute to a healthier lifestyle and planet.

Newer and Speedier Electric Bike

Our electric bikes provide an easy and quick option when choosing your travel. Go from one destination to the next with ease using our greener alternative, reducing your carbon footprint.

How do we work?

BunnyHop uses a few different ways of getting started. You can easily scan the QR codes found on the bike to set up your account via the app. We work as a loyality program in which you can use your account online to top up your spending. We also offer a loyality card so that you can top up a physical card. This means that our bikes aren't limited to card only users. We understand that sometimes the use of card only options can retrict our users, and here at BunnyHop we are actively trying get as many healthy minds involved.

1

Download the app. Your account, your progress, information and friends will be kept in the app.

2

Sign up. This means when you need to use a bike, you can get going with ease.

3

Scan your bike. To connect your bike to your account you will need to use the built in QR scanner. If you are paying with your loyality card you can tap it on the indication on the side of the bike. This means that your app can track where you ride, how long you ride, how fast you ride. Not only for payment reasons but also to keep track of your progress. Our app uses your progress to calculate your contribution to reducing CO2 emissons, calculate your calories burned.

4

To get started you can choose one of two ways to set up your account. You can pay with card through the app, or you can grab a loyality card from your nearby off-license and pay in cash.

5

GET MOVING! Nothing is stopping you now. All you need to do is get pedalling!
